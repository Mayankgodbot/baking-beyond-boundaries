System Role & Objective:
Act as an expert Full-Stack Developer, Mobile App Developer, and UI/UX Designer. Your objective is to architect, design, and outline the development of a complete digital ecosystem (Website + iOS/Android Mobile Application) for a premium bakery and custom cake business named "Baking Beyond Boundaries." This business currently operates heavily via Instagram. The goal of this project is to create a professional, independent platform that streamlines custom orders, showcases their portfolio, handles e-commerce sales, and provides a seamless user experience for their clients.

1. Brand Identity & UI/UX Guidelines
Vibe & Aesthetic: Warm, modern, appetizing, and premium. The design should feel artisanal but highly professional.

Color Palette: Soft pastels (creams, warm vanilla, soft blush pink, or mint) contrasted with rich, deep tones (like dark chocolate brown or charcoal) for text readability.

Typography: An elegant serif font for headings (to give a premium bakery feel) combined with a clean, modern sans-serif for body text.

Animations: Smooth, lightweight animations. Use lazy loading for high-res cake images, soft fade-ins on scroll, and micro-interactions (e.g., buttons subtly scaling up on hover/tap, cart icon bouncing when an item is added).

Media Focus: High-quality imagery is the core of the site. Integrate a live, stylized Instagram feed to pull recent creations automatically.

2. Website Requirements (E-commerce & Portfolio)
The website must be fully responsive (Mobile, Tablet, Desktop) and SEO-optimized.

Core Pages & Features:

Home Page: High-quality hero video/image slider of best-selling cakes, "Order Now" CTA, featured products, client testimonials, and a live Instagram feed.

Menu / Shop: E-commerce catalog divided into categories (e.g., Custom Cakes, Cupcakes, Pastries, Festive Specials). Must include filtering and search capabilities.

Custom Order Builder (Crucial): A dedicated, interactive multi-step form for bespoke cake inquiries:

Step 1: Cake Size & Tiers.

Step 2: Sponge Flavor & Fillings.

Step 3: Theme/Design description.

Step 4: Image Upload (for reference designs).

Step 5: Date picker for delivery/pickup.

About Us: The story behind "Baking Beyond Boundaries," the chef’s background, and the philosophy of the ingredients used.

Contact & FAQ: Location, operating hours, delivery radius, refund policies, and a contact form.

User Account: Order history, saved delivery addresses, and wishlists.

3. Mobile Application Requirements (iOS & Android)
The mobile app should be built for customer retention, ease of ordering, and loyalty.

App-Specific Features:

Frictionless Onboarding: Login via Email, Google, or Apple ID.

Push Notifications: Real-time updates on order status (e.g., "Your cake is in the oven!", "Out for delivery"), and promotional alerts for flash sales or holiday specials.

Mobile-Optimized Checkout: Integration with Apple Pay, Google Pay, UPI (if applicable), and major credit cards for 1-click checkout.

Loyalty/Rewards Program: A point-based system (e.g., "Earn 1 point for every $1 spent") that users can track in a visual dashboard and redeem for discounts to encourage app retention.

Order Tracking: A visual timeline for custom orders (Order Placed -> Baking -> Decorating -> Ready for Pickup/Delivery).

App-Only Exclusives: Secret menu items or early access to holiday bookings available only to app users.

4. Admin Panel & Dashboard (For the Client/Owner)
The backend must be user-friendly for the bakery owner to manage daily operations without needing coding knowledge.

Order Management: A Kanban-style board (New Orders, In Progress, Ready, Completed) to track the baking schedule.

Inventory & Menu Management: Ability to easily add/remove products, mark items as "Sold Out," and update prices.

Custom Quote Generator: An interface to review custom order requests, price them, and send an invoice directly to the customer's email/app.

Analytics: Sales reports, most popular flavors, peak order times, and app vs. web revenue splits.

5. Technical Stack Recommendations (Please confirm or suggest alternatives)
Frontend Web: Next.js (React) or Vue.js for a blazing-fast, SEO-friendly experience.

Mobile App: Flutter or React Native (for a single codebase deploying to both iOS and Android).

Backend & CMS: Node.js / Express or a headless CMS like Strapi or Shopify Plus (if leveraging existing e-commerce infrastructure).

Database: PostgreSQL or MongoDB.

Cloud Storage: AWS S3 or Cloudinary (for fast loading of high-resolution cake images).

Payment Gateway: Stripe, Razorpay, or Square integration.

Deliverables Required from Developer:

Wireframes and UI mockups (Figma) for both Web and Mobile.

Database schema architecture.

A phased development roadmap (e.g., Phase 1: Web MVP, Phase 2: Mobile App, Phase 3: Loyalty Program).
